What is DK Bananza?

DK Bananza (Donkey Kong Bananza) is a groundbreaking 2025 platform game for the Nintendo Switch 2 that revolutionizes the Donkey Kong franchise with fully destructible environments. Players control the iconic gorilla as he ventures underground with young Pauline to recover stolen Banandium Gems from villainous apes.

The game features a unique skill tree system where players earn Banandium Gems to invest in different upgrade paths: Power, Speed, Destruction, and Transformation. Each path unlocks special abilities that drastically change gameplay. Transform into creatures like the Ostrich for speed and egg bombs, or the Zebra for enhanced strength. Our Skill Calculator tool helps you plan optimal builds for any playstyle, whether you prefer smashing through terrain, speedrunning through levels, or mastering transformations.

Revolutionary Gameplay Features

DK Bananza stands out as the most innovative evolution of the Donkey Kong franchise. Unlike traditional platformers, DK Bananza introduces a fully destructible voxel-based environment system where players can smash through almost any surface, rip pieces from walls, and create new paths through the underground world.

The game's underground world is divided into unique themed layers, including ice, lava, and tropical biomes. Each area presents distinctive challenges requiring strategic use of Donkey Kong's abilities. The terrain destruction mechanics allow for creative problem-solving - dig underground passages, stack soft terrain to reach new heights, or throw environmental objects as weapons. With sandbox-style exploration similar to Super Mario Odyssey but with the added dimension of environmental manipulation, DK Bananza delivers a fresh take on the platforming genre.
